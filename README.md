# 🕯️ Candlelit AI: Pro Crypto Terminal

> **Empowering retail traders with GenAI-driven market insights and sentiment analysis.**

---

## 🚀 The Product Vision
Most crypto terminals provide data, but not **understanding**. Retail traders are often overwhelmed by complex candles and indicators. **Candlelit AI** bridges this gap by using a logic-driven AI layer to interpret market patterns into plain English.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Contextual Pattern Recognition:** Instead of just showing a chart, the AI identifies specific formations like *Double Bottoms (W)* or *Head & Shoulders* and explains the reasoning.
* **Market Sentiment Pulse:** A visual Fear/Greed index that aggregates market "mood" for faster decision-making.
* **Actionable Intelligence:** Provides clear Bullish/Bearish signals based on technical support and resistance levels.
* **Premium UX:** Built with a "dark-mode first" aesthetic to reduce eye strain during long trading sessions.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Custom Grid & Flexbox), JavaScript (ES6+)
* **Data Visualization:** TradingView Advanced Charts Integration
* **Design System:** Plus Jakarta Sans Typography
